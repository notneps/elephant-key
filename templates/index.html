What

Elephant Key is a password generator that generates strong but easy to remember passwords. Every time you reload this page you get a new password. Elephants are strong and never forget! We want passwords that are both strong and memorable.

Why

While in theory, the best passwords are completely random strings (e.g., “szH`"Kk63^.4R=Bzt”), the best type of strong passwords in practice are the passwords that people will actually use. I’ve found that people won’t use strong passwords if it means they have a hard time remembering them or typing them in.

How

When you load this page, a simple Python program randomly selects three words from a list of over 58,000 English words. Boom: strong but easy to remember password!

Do you know my password now?

Nope, I don’t know your password. This site doesn’t keep any logs. There are no records of the passwords generated. In other words, have no idea:

  • what passwords this website generated for you;
  • who you are;
  • where you plan on using these passwords; or
  • whether you use them at all.

But you definitely shouldn’t take my word for it. You can tweak or change the passwords generated here to make them more unique and memorable to you.
